Summary

WordJig: Watch Puzzle is a Wear OS word puzzle game published by aminhanifm. The game works entirely offline and does not require a developer-operated account. The app does not collect or transmit personal information or gameplay data. Information needed to provide game progress, statistics, and preferences is stored only in the app's private storage on the user's watch.

Information stored on the device

WordJig may store the following information locally on the watch:

  • the haptic-feedback preference;
  • the current puzzle and its progress;
  • recent puzzle identifiers used to vary puzzle selection;
  • Daily Jig completion dates and streaks; and
  • aggregate gameplay statistics, such as puzzles solved, best time, stars, perfect puzzles, and hints used.

This information is not associated with a developer account or sent to the developer. Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer are disabled for WordJig's app data.

Data collection and transmission

WordJig does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or use personal information. It contains no advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, tracking, social, account, or cloud-sync SDK. It does not communicate with the developer or any third-party server.

Google Play purchase and distribution

Google Play processes the purchase, download, licensing, refund, and related platform activity for the paid app. WordJig itself does not receive or process payment-card details and contains no in-app purchase or subscription system.

Google may provide the developer with sales, order, financial, and device compatibility reports through Google Play Console for distribution, support, refunds, tax, and fraud-prevention purposes. These records are created and controlled by Google Play, not collected or transmitted by the WordJig app. Google's handling of this information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions and device access

WordJig requests no Android permissions. In particular, it has no internet access and does not access location, contacts, photos, files, microphone, camera, health information, nearby devices, or advertising identifiers. Haptic feedback is performed locally by the watch and can be turned off in the game settings.

Sharing and sale of information

The developer does not receive WordJig gameplay data or personal information and therefore does not share or sell it. WordJig has no third-party advertising or analytics partners.

Data retention and deletion

Local data remains on the watch while WordJig is installed. The in-app Reset statistics action clears scores, streaks, recent-puzzle history, and Daily Jig completion history. It preserves the haptic preference and any current unfinished puzzle.

To delete all WordJig data, clear the app's storage in Android settings or uninstall the app. Because the app sends no data to the developer, there is no remote WordJig account or gameplay database to delete. Google retains and deletes Play purchase records according to its own policies, account controls, and legal obligations.

Security

WordJig uses Android app-private storage for its local data. The app has no network permission, so it cannot transmit that data over the internet. Users should still protect their watch and Google account with the security controls available on their devices.

Children's privacy

WordJig is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13 or the applicable minimum digital-consent age in their country. The app does not collect personal information from children or adults. A parent or guardian with a question can use the contact details below.
